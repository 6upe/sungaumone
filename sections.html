Zambia & Angola Project

AIM

ZAMANGRIC PROJECT is aiming to deliver the dual objectives of competitive returns for shareholders, investors, partners, and maximum socio-economic benefits to the general Zambian landscape. The driving motivation was that it was evident, that the high levels of unemployment and poverty in Zambia and Angola were having devastating effects on the Zambian and Angolan citizen.

Resilient Income Improvement

ZAMANGRIC PROJECT seeks among other things to improve incomes, resilience of the informal sector & farmers and small-scale entrepreneurs by increasing production and productivity across selected sectors.

ZAMANGRIC PROJECT was established by a group of Zambian visionaries and entrepreneurs to use profitable enterprise as a vehicle for significantly contributing to the social-economic dynamics of Zambia.
