About Our Founder

Dr. Roberto Chiteta is an academic, visionary and youthful entrepreneur. He is Ambassador of Sunga Umone Group and also serves as Chief Relationship Officer at GIC Capital Corp. Dr. Chiteta had the vision of creating opportunities for individual and community empowerment by establishing SUNGA UMONE GROUP Through his vast network the business SUG model aims to find Zambia AND ANGOLA. equity partners, sponsors and qualified staff to tap into local talent Entrepreneus.
The talents will be groomed to become professional Entrepreneurial who will become inspirations in the communities they come. Consequently, they become drivers for other endeavors all aimed to mutually benefit the company, the local farmers, Entrepreneur etc as well as the communities. Through the influence of the good farming', it is hoped that the community will benefit from various opportunities through projects that will come with the corporatives corporate social responsibility (CSR). Among these it is hoped that the community will benefit from agriculture, education , Construction, health and other sports. The Company is also seeking equity partners to be drivers of this dream with benefits ranging from being part of a thriving youth development platform, be custodians and agents for players, experience coaching education from academy to full professional and also enjoy the benefits and access to use SUG intellectual property.
Dr. Chiteta holds a Degree in Business Administration from the UK, A Master's Degree in Geotechnical Engineering from China; and a PHD in Criminal Justice from Washington, United States of America. He is also a competent project manager and a certified CISCO Licentiate from the University of Zambia. His skill set encompasses start-ups, business development, marketing business strategy, business planning, finance, entrepreneurship, new business development and customer relationship management among others...

Zambia & Angola Project

AIM

ZAMANGRIC PROJECT is aiming to deliver the dual objectives of competitive returns for shareholders, investors, partners, and maximum socio-economic benefits to the general Zambian landscape. The driving motivation was that it was evident, that the high levels of unemployment and poverty in Zambia and Angola were having devastating effects on the Zambian and Angolan citizen.

Investments

ZAMANGRIC has planned investments of over five years (2023-2035) for phase one interventions to accelerate the development of financial inclusion through it’s, commercializing small scale farmers through the implementation of a agriculture, food processing , marketable and sustainable agricultural value chains in Zambia.

Resilient Income Improvement

ZAMANGRIC PROJECT seeks among other things to improve incomes, resilience of the informal sector & farmers and small-scale entrepreneurs by increasing production and productivity across selected sectors.

ZAMANGRIC PROJECT was established by a group of Zambian visionaries and entrepreneurs to use profitable enterprise as a vehicle for significantly contributing to the social-economic dynamics of Zambia.
